ONE OK ROCK about to rock out the Midwest

Looking for a rock concert to jam out to? Well look no further! ONE OK ROCK, a Japanese rock band composed of four members, are now touring the US and bringing to audiences live their dynamic music.

Although formed in Japan, their music style is dynamic and appeals a vast network of foreign fans. With each album, their music style changes, showing listeners everywhere that despite being in the scene for so long, they aren't afraid to experiment and add new touches to a previously niche genre.

B.A.P. bringing their Absolute Best upon the stage

B.A.P. fans rejoice! After a long hiatus starting in 2014, the six men of B.A.P. are back to bring fans their unique quality of music worldwide! For those who aren't quite as familiar with B.A.P., no worries! With their raw raps and soothing vocals, their music style has no limits and appeals to audiences of different tastes.

With leader Bang Yong Guk's gritty raps, Zelo's high-speed raps, and Daehyun's and Youngjae's powerful vocal performance, their musical ability is top-notch without a doubt. With such a variety in voices, including member Himchan and Jongup's unique vocal sounds, B.A.P. isn't afraid to push the envelope when it comes to music.
